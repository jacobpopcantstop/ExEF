Getting Started

How to get started

A clear path from naming the problem to getting matched with the right support — whether you're an adult, a professional, or a family.

Identify the friction

Use a free assessment tool to name the challenge before booking anything. The ESQ-R, time blindness calibrator, and task-start friction tool help clarify what is actually going on.

Schedule a consultation

A free 20-minute call to discuss fit, goals, and which service lane matches your needs. No pressure, no upsell.

Get matched to support

EF coaching, occupational therapy, or educational specialist — each with a clear scope, session plan, and measurable goals.

Track progress together

Regular check-ins, practical goals, and transparent communication. Coaching works best when everyone can see what is changing.
