About Expert EF

Executive function coaching grounded in neuroscience, built for adults, professionals, and students. We believe EF challenges deserve science-grounded support — not shame, not productivity hacks, not "just try harder."

Explore Our Services View Our Curriculum

Our Approach

ExEF combines the executive function frameworks of Barkley, Brown, Dawson & Guare, and Ward into one coherent coaching model. Every session, assessment, and tool references a named research framework — not generic advice.

Framework-First

Coaching grounded in named neuropsychological models, not vague tips or willpower-based approaches.

All Ages

From adults recently diagnosed with ADHD to professionals and students — we meet people where they are.

Open Curriculum

Our educational materials are publicly available. We charge for expert review and credentialing, not access to knowledge.

Our Team

JR

Jacob Rozansky

Founder • ICF-Certified Coach • ADDCA ADHD Coaching (in progress)

I started Expert EF because I saw a gap between what the research says about executive function and the support most people actually get. Too often, EF challenges get reduced to "try harder" or "just use a planner." That's not how the brain works.

Coaching grounded in the right frameworks meets people in context — at the point of performance, not in the abstract. That's what ExEF is built to do.
