Free ESQ-R Interactive Intake

ExEF's live ESQ-R tool gives you a fast, structured read on five executive-function areas, then points you to the next useful tool or support path.

Back to Assessment Hub Open Cross-Signal Profile

What This Measures

Planning and time management, task initiation and follow-through, attention and working memory, organization and self-monitoring, and emotional regulation and flexibility.

Best First Use

Use ESQ-R first if you want a broad, non-diagnostic intake pattern before choosing a more specific ExEF tool or a paid service.

How To Answer

Rate each statement from 1 to 5 based on how you usually function, not on your best day. Higher scores mean stronger current capacity in that area — the opposite of friction-style scales like the EF Profile Story, so don't compare raw numbers across tools.

What You Get

A clearer result narrative, two current strengths, two leverage points, and a recommended path into the other ExEF tools.

Important framing

This tool is for self-awareness, planning, and coaching-style reflection. It is not a diagnosis and should not be treated as one.
