Quick Questions

These are the objections and logistics families usually want clarified before they book.

Do you offer a trial session?

Yes—your first paid session is a "trial" at the regular rate. If it's not a fit, there's no obligation to continue.

How soon can we start?

Availability varies, but initial sessions can usually be scheduled within 1-2 weeks of the consultation call.

Do you work with schools?

EFI can communicate with teachers and learning specialists as needed, with permission. IEP meeting attendance is scoped as a separate service.

What if my child doesn't want coaching?

This is common. Part of EFI's design is building buy-in with reluctant students. Many warm up quickly once they realize this is not just more tutoring.
