# EFI OT Review Brief

## What EFI is

The Executive Functioning Institute is an asynchronous executive functioning coaching certification and coaching platform. The site includes:

- a Pathway curriculum
- public educational resources
- executive functioning tools and assessments
- certification and coaching pages

EFI is not intended to present itself as therapy, diagnosis, or clinical treatment. This review is to help tighten that boundary and improve the clinical precision of the site's public language.

## What I’d like your help reviewing

Please review the site for:

- clinical accuracy
- coaching vs therapy boundaries
- referral clarity
- special-populations language
- practical usefulness for real families, students, and professionals
- any wording that overstates what coaching can do

## Questions to keep in mind

1. Does any page sound like diagnosis or treatment when it should sound like coaching, education, or skill support?
2. Are referral triggers clear enough?
3. Are there claims that feel too broad, too confident, or not well-bounded?
4. Are there places where OT-informed language around environment, function, transitions, sensory load, or school/home implementation would make the guidance better?
5. Are special-populations references accurate and careful enough?

## Easiest way to give feedback

For each note, just send:

- page name
- quote or section heading
- what feels off
- what you’d change
- why

Example:

- `module-5.html`
- `Special populations` section
- Current wording risks sounding too generalized for autistic learners
- Suggest changing X to Y
- Better reflects variability and avoids implying a single intervention path
