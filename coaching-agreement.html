ExEF Coaching Agreement (Beta Sprint)

1) Scope

Executive function coaching and practical skills support only.

2) Not Therapy/Diagnosis/Treatment

Coaching is not psychotherapy, diagnosis, medical treatment, crisis care, or legal/academic documentation support.

3) Confidentiality and Limits

Session details remain private except where disclosure is legally required or necessary for safety.

4) Payment

Beta Sprint package is 4 sessions for $200 (first 5 clients); payment due before Session 1.

5) Cancellation/Rescheduling

24-hour notice requested; late cancellations may be counted as used sessions.

6) Between-session Communication

Light async communication is available for implementation questions.

7) Client Responsibility

Client is responsible for attending sessions, trying agreed experiments, and sharing what happened honestly.

8) Referral-out Conditions

Coaching pauses/refer-out applies for crisis, severe clinical needs, diagnosis requests, or treatment-level concerns.

9) Pause/Termination

Either party may pause or end coaching if fit, safety, or scope concerns arise.
