Getting Started

How families get started

A clear path from naming the problem to getting matched with the right support.

Identify the friction

Use a free assessment tool to name the challenge before booking anything. The ESQ-R, time blindness calibrator, and task-start friction tool help clarify what is actually going on.

Schedule a consultation

A free 20-minute call to discuss fit, goals, and which service lane matches your family's needs. No pressure, no upsell.

Get matched to support

EF coaching, occupational therapy, or educational specialist — each with a clear scope, session plan, and measurable goals.

Track progress together

Regular check-ins, practical goals, and transparent communication with families. Coaching works best when everyone can see what is changing.

Why EFI

Why families choose EFI

Named Frameworks, Not Generic Advice

Coaching grounded in Barkley, Brown, Dawson & Guare, and Ward — not vague productivity tips. Every session references a named model.

School-System Fluency

Understanding of IEPs, 504s, transition planning, and how schools actually work. EFI helps families navigate district language and advocate effectively.

Local and Virtual Options

In-person sessions in San Diego with virtual availability for families everywhere. Same quality, same frameworks, flexible delivery.

Assessment Before Sales

Free tools and guided diagnostics help families understand the problem before any paid service begins. Signal first, then support.
