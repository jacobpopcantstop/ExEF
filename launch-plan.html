90-Day Coaching Business Launch Plan

A week-by-week action plan covering niche selection, pricing, first-client outreach, and referral pipeline setup — built specifically for new EF and ADHD coaches.

Inside the plan

  • Weeks 1–2: Niche definition, ICP statement, and pricing floor
  • Weeks 3–5: First-client outreach scripts and discovery-call structure
  • Weeks 6–8: Service packaging, intake artifacts, session cadence
  • Weeks 9–12: Referral pipeline, testimonial loop, scope-of-practice review

Designed to pair with the Scope of Practice Guide and the Coach Directory listing process.
