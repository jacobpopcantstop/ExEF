Sale Calendar

When EFI Runs Promotional Weeks

EFI does not run always-on discounts. Promotional pricing is reserved for predictable demand spikes so the standard list price stays clear and credible.

Parents

  • Late July to early September for back-to-school demand
  • Early January for semester reset and New Year planning
  • Mid May to late June for finals, summer planning, and transitions
  • Short 5-7 day windows around report cards, IEP seasons, or school breaks

Educators

  • June to July for career-transition and summer planning
  • Late October to mid November for midyear burnout and intervention demand
  • Launch-week offers tied to webinars, workshops, or cohort email courses

Students

  • Early January for academic reset momentum
  • Mid May to late June for finals and summer transition planning
  • Short launch windows tied to workshops or seasonal skill-building events

Campaign Rules

EFI should generally run 3-6 promotional weeks per year, with each campaign lasting about 5-10 days. Public list pricing remains visible as the anchor before and after every sale period.
