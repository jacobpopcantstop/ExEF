Diagnostic Stack

Choose One Tool, or Complete the Full Sequence

Each free tool answers a different everyday question. Start with the one that sounds most like your problem, then add more only if you want a fuller picture.

ESQ-R Executive Functioning Test

Best first stop if you are not sure what is wrong yet. It gives you a broad read on which executive function areas are most strained.

Take ESQ-R

Executive Functioning Environment Quiz

Use this if you keep wondering why everything feels harder in your actual day. It shows whether your space, tools, and routines are helping or hurting you.

Take Environment Quiz

EF Profile Story Quiz

Use this if you want a result that feels more human than clinical. It turns your patterns into a readable story and gives you a 7-day experiment to try.

Take EF Profile Story

Time Blindness Calibrator

Use this if you are always surprised by how long things take. It helps you spot your planning blind spots and build a more realistic time estimate.

Open Calibrator

Task Start Friction Diagnostic

Use this when the issue is not knowing what to do, but actually starting. It turns your stuck point into a concrete start plan for the next task.

Run Diagnostic

Conative Action Profile

Use this if you want to understand how you naturally take action. It shows the kind of work style and momentum pattern you default to under pressure.

Take Action Profile

Best Fit Fitness Quiz

Answer 20 preference and lifestyle questions, then get a ranked list of 30 exercise formats likely to fit your real life. Consistent movement is one of the strongest supports for EF stability.

Take Fitness Quiz

Sleep & Executive Function Quiz

Use this to check whether poor sleep is quietly amplifying focus, planning, and emotional regulation struggles, then get a practical weekly reset plan.

Take Sleep Quiz
