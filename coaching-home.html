Occupational Therapy

OT services that target the body-brain connection

Led by Cole M., PhD, OTR/L — launching Fall 2026. Join the interest list below to be first in line for intake.

OT Evaluation

$395 / 90–120 min

Comprehensive evaluation covering sensory processing, motor planning, praxis, visual-motor integration, and daily living skills. Includes written report.

  • Standardized assessments as indicated
  • Functional observation and parent/adult interview
  • Written report with treatment priorities

Sensory Integration Therapy

$165 / 50 min

Weekly or twice-weekly sensory integration sessions for children and adults whose regulation challenges are driving the EF picture.

  • Individualized sensory diet design
  • Home and school / work recommendations
  • Progress review every 12 sessions

Self-Regulation Coaching

$155 / 50 min

Targeted OT sessions for emotional regulation, arousal modulation, and the interoceptive skills that underlie follow-through and attention.

  • Interoception and self-cue training
  • Co-regulation scripts for parents or partners
  • Written tools for use between sessions

Daily Living Skills Program

$640 / 4-week block

Structured program targeting morning routines, meal prep, laundry, hygiene, or other ADLs that are limiting independence at home or in a dorm.

  • Four 60-minute in-home or telehealth sessions
  • Visual supports and routine charts
  • Family coaching session included

Fine & Visual-Motor Intervention

$155 / 50 min

Handwriting, keyboarding, and visual-motor skill development for students and adults where motor output is the bottleneck, not the thinking.

  • Goal-based tracking with quarterly re-test
  • Classroom or workplace accommodations memo
  • Assistive technology recommendations

School or Workplace Consultation

$275 / 75 min

On-site or virtual consultation to design environmental supports, sensory-friendly spaces, and regulation-supportive schedules.

  • Environmental walkthrough and audit
  • Written recommendations memo
