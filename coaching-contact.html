How soon can we start?

Availability varies, but initial sessions can usually be scheduled within 1–2 weeks of the consultation call.

Do you work with schools?

EFI can communicate with teachers and learning specialists as needed, with permission. IEP meeting attendance is scoped as a separate service.

What if my child does not want coaching?

This is common. Part of EFI's design builds buy-in with reluctant students. Many warm up quickly once they realize this is not just more tutoring.
