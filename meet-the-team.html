ExEF + Referral Network

One coach. A short list of independent referrals.

ExEF is a solo practice led by Jacob Rozansky. The educators and clinicians below are independent contractors and recommended referrals — not ExEF employees. We list them so when something falls outside coaching scope, you have a vetted name to call.

Why it matters: keeping clinicians off ExEF’s payroll keeps licensing, liability, and scope-of-practice lines clean. You hire each professional directly under their own credentials and terms.
