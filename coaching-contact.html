Before You Book

Common questions before the first call.

How soon can we start?

Beta Sprint spots are currently open at $200 for 4 sessions (first 5 clients). The fit call confirms scope, goals, and whether coaching is the right next step.

Do you work with schools?

ExEF can communicate with teachers and learning specialists as needed, with permission. IEP meeting attendance is scoped as a separate service.

What if my child does not want coaching?

This is common. Part of ExEF's design builds buy-in with reluctant students. Many warm up quickly once they realize this is not just more tutoring.
