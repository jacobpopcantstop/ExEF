Meet the Team

The people behind the support lanes

Three practitioners. Three lanes. Each with a clear scope, so the fit question stays honest.

JR

Jacob Rozansky

Founder · EF & ADHD coach

Jacob works with adults navigating late ADHD diagnosis, professionals stalling on initiation and follow-through, and students building real independence. His approach is grounded in Barkley, Brown, Dawson & Guare, and Ward — translated into systems people can actually run.

  • Adult ADHD and late-diagnosis coaching
  • Founder and professional EF systems
  • Assessment synthesis and case formulation
  • Coaching supervision and certification support
DB

Diamond B.

Special educator · Educational strategy and advocacy

Diamond works at the intersection of school systems, family communication, and student independence. She is especially strong when execution breaks down across home, school, and transition planning.

  • IEP and 504 translation for families
  • Student transition and school-home systems
  • Learning strategies and academic routines
  • Parent-teacher communication planning
CM

Cole M.

PhD · Occupational therapist

Cole brings the sensory, motor, and daily-living lens. His work is especially useful when regulation, transitions, motor planning, or the body-environment relationship are driving the EF problem.

  • Sensory regulation and environmental fit
  • Daily living routines and transitions
  • Motor-planning and embodied EF supports
  • OT-informed home and school strategy consults
