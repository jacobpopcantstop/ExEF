## What was implemented

### 1) Outcome mapping added on coaching home
A new outcomes strip now links session mechanics to concrete school/family outcomes:
- start-friction drills → earlier assignment initiation
- missing-work recovery routine → fewer zeros and clearer catch-up plans
- parent-student planning scripts → lower homework conflict

### 2) Mechanism proof added for each method
Each method card now includes a concise case example so families can see mechanism → observable behavior change:
- improv: shutdown recovery under error pressure
- chess: pause-before-action transfer to schoolwork
- cubing: algorithmic sequencing for multi-step assignments

### 3) Lead pre-qualification added
A “Not a fit (or not yet)” block now sets boundaries and expectations around:
- tutoring-only requests with no EF behavior change goals
- lack of between-session practice commitment
- cases requiring higher clinical support than coaching scope

### 4) Cross-page positioning alignment completed
Niche framing (“creative EF”, “creative-to-structured”) now appears on:
- `coaching-home.html`
- `coaching-contact.html`
- `coaching-services.html`

This reduces ad-to-call messaging drift and keeps consultation expectations aligned with the core offer.

## Current positioning statement
“EFI helps creative, neurodivergent students turn improvisational strengths into reliable executive-function systems for school and life.”
