Our Approach

ExEF combines the executive function frameworks of Barkley, Brown, Dawson & Guare, and Ward for a grounded and effective coaching model. All resources and services reference named research frameworks with practicality at the core.

Framework-First

Coaching grounded in named models, systems, and support frameworks.

All Ages

From adults recently diagnosed with ADHD to professionals and students — we meet people where they are.

ExEF + Referral Network

ExEF is a solo coaching practice. The educator and OT below are independent contractors and recommended referrals, not ExEF employees — you engage them directly under their own credentials.

JR

Jacob Rozansky

Founder • Completing ADDCA ADHD coach training (ICF & PAAC accredited program)

I started ExEF because I saw a gap between what the research says about executive function and the support most people actually get. Too often, EF challenges get reduced to "try harder" or "just use a planner." The brain works differently than that.

Coaching grounded in the right frameworks meets people in context — at the point of performance, in real life. My ADDCA training is part of that standard: ADHD-specific coaching skills, clear scope boundaries, and practical support that fits the nervous system people actually have.
