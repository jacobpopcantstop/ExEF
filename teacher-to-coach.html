Educator Sale Windows

ExEF keeps list pricing public and uses short promotional windows when educator demand predictably spikes.

  • June to July for summer transition planning
  • Late October to mid November for midyear burnout and intervention demand
  • Short launch weeks around webinars, workshops, and educator email cohorts

These campaigns should stay limited to about 5-10 days so the $695 enrollment anchor and premium coaching positioning remain intact.
