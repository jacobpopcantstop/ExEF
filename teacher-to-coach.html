What the Transition Actually Looks Like

Moving from education to coaching is real work. Here is what the early phase typically involves:

  • Building your first 3–5 client relationships takes most coaches 2–4 months
  • Session rates in EF coaching vary widely by region and experience — research your local market
  • Most new coaches start part-time while maintaining other income
  • The credential helps with positioning, but your client results build the practice

EFI does not make income projections. Your results depend on your market, effort, prior experience, and client outcomes.

Educator Sale Windows

EFI keeps list pricing public and uses short promotional windows when educator demand predictably spikes.

  • June to July for summer transition planning
  • Late October to mid November for midyear burnout and intervention demand
  • Short launch weeks around webinars, workshops, and educator email cohorts

These campaigns should stay limited to about 5-10 days so the $695 enrollment anchor and premium coaching positioning remain intact.
